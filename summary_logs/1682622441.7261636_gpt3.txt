Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson were called to Pondicherry Lodge in Upper Norwood to investigate the death of Bartholomew Sholto. They found that a valuable collection of Indian gems had been stolen. A fortunate coincidence led to the arrival of Mr. Athelney Jones, a member of the detective police force, who was able to quickly begin his investigation of the criminals. In the meantime, Holmes dispatched a wire to his lieutenant, Wiggins, and his gang to assist in the case. The story was reported in the Standard newspaper.
